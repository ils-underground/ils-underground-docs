ils-underground¶

This site aims to provide a place to post help, examples (code samples, scripts), short presentations, etc–related to the topic of working with the ILS on a technical-level

The github org page for this group can be found here: https://github.com/ils-underground

(something here about how to reach out to people involved with any of this–maybe a way to connect the the slack channel, idk?)

This resource is, “use at your own risk”. If you feel something is wrong, or lacking please feel free to contribute–more info is available below on how to do so!
